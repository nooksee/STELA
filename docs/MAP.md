## 5. The Shipping Spine (Operator Chain)
*End-to-end delivery chain from topic to merge.*

`TOPIC.md` Ôćĺ `PLAN.md` Ôćĺ `storage/dp/intake/DP.md` Ôćĺ execution Ôćĺ `RESULTS` + `CLOSING` Ôćĺ audit bundle Ôćĺ merge

* **Topic:** `storage/handoff/TOPIC.md` ÔÇö latest-wins analyst input.
* **Plan:** `storage/handoff/PLAN.md` ÔÇö latest-wins analyst output, architect input.
* **Active DP draft:** `storage/dp/intake/DP.md` ÔÇö latest-wins operator surface.
* **Packet/process identity:** `DP-OPS-XXXX` ÔÇö retained in the TASK leaf chain for base packets, addendum lineage leaves for addenda, certify receipts, and telemetry.
* **Certify:** `ops/bin/certify` ÔÇö generates `RESULTS`, emits SoP/PoW/TASK archive leaves, requires `STELA_TRACE_ID` from latest OPEN artifact.
* **Audit bundle:** `storage/handoff/AUDIT-*.txt` (initial) / `storage/handoff/AUDIT-R*.txt` (rerun) ÔÇö generated by `ops/bin/bundle --profile=audit`.
* **Merge:** Operator commits work branch, opens PR per `CLOSING` sidecar, merges to main.
* **Secondary lanes:** `foreman/addendum` (intervention), `conform/conformist` (normalization), `execution-decision` (disposable/manual) ÔÇö bounded sidepaths, not replacements for RESULTS or audit truth.
* **Delivery mechanics:** `docs/MANUAL.md`.

## 5. Unified Jurisdiction
*Policy of Truth governs all scopes without parallel authority.*
* **Single Authority:** PoT is the sole authority across `ops/`, `docs/`, `opt/`, and `projects/`.
* **Project Position:** Projects are payloads operating under PoT, not a separate provider or consumer tier.
